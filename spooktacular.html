Spooktacular

Spooktacular is an annual event held every year on the last Friday before Halloween. Since its inception 10 years ago, where we had just over 200 participants, the race has grown into one of the biggest events in the country. Last year we had 1600 running through the streets of Enniskillen. With the increased popularity, the level of competition has increased too year on year. Our course record holders are Gideon Komosomp (15:03) and Denise Toner (18:30). Unfortunately, due to Covid we could not hold our race this year in what would have been our 10th year running it.
